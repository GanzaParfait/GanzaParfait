# FUTURE_PAGES_BUILD_PLAN.md

This document outlines the step-by-step implementation plan for the secondary pages of `princeparfait.com`. Each page description includes real information derived from the brand messaging and architecture, detailing what needs to be built beyond the homepage.

## 1. About Page (`/about`)
**Objective:** A deep dive into the person behind the impact.

*   **Story & Journey Section:**
    *   Build a vertical timeline or scroll-driven narrative component highlighting key milestones.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* The specific dates and narrative paragraphs of Prince's journey from early career to his current strategic role.
*   **Principles & Values Grid:**
    *   Premium card layout detailing core ethical and professional drivers.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* 3-5 core principles (e.g., "Execution over theory", "Data-driven decisions").
*   **Vision Statement:**
    *   A prominent typographic section for long-term goals and strategic focus.

## 2. Impact Page (`/impact`)
**Objective:** The proof of value, demonstrating real, measurable outcomes.

*   **Impact Metrics Header:**
    *   Large, animated number counters for key statistics (e.g., "Organizations Served", "Systems Delivered", "Students Trained").
*   **Organizations Served List:**
    *   A clean, list-based UI with client names and short summaries.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* Complete list of clients (Lerony, Caritas Rwanda, Ethical Research Solutions, APN, etc.).
*   **Consulting & Training Highlights:**
    *   Sections detailing specific engagements and research contributions.

## 3. Experience Page (`/experience`)
**Objective:** A curated, professional timeline highlighting leadership and execution.

*   **Professional Timeline Component:**
    *   An interactive vertical timeline.
    *   Each entry must explicitly structure: **Challenge**, **Contribution**, **Outcome**, **Evidence**.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* Detailed paragraphs for Lerony Co. Ltd, Ethical Research Solutions, Caritas Rwanda, and APN African Marketplace following the C-C-O-E framework.

## 4. Projects (Case Studies) Page (`/projects`)
**Objective:** A comprehensive case-study collection (not a standard developer gallery).

*   **Case Study Index Grid:**
    *   Large, high-quality thumbnails with premium hover effects.
*   **Individual Case Study Template (`/projects/[id]`):**
    *   Must include sections: Context, Challenge, Approach, Solution, Outcome, Lessons Learned, Screenshots.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* High-resolution screenshots of the Inventory Management System, CRNIS, and APN Marketplace, plus full written narratives for each.

## 5. Knowledge & Expertise Page (`/expertise`)
**Objective:** A catalog of competencies and high-level domains.

*   **Domains Accordion/Tabs:**
    *   A clean, interactive UI to browse knowledge areas without overwhelming the user.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* Short descriptive text for each domain (Organizational Transformation, Business Operations, Digital Strategy, Data Collection & Analysis, etc.).
*   **Professional Skills Matrix:**
    *   A typographic layout (no skill bars) listing actionable capabilities like Strategic Planning, Project Leadership, and Process Mapping.

## 6. Evidence Page (`/evidence`)
**Objective:** The definitive proof library that substantiates all claims.

*   **Masonry Grid / Filterable Portfolio:**
    *   Categories: Systems, Leadership, Organizations, Recognition.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* Actual client logos, PDF recommendation letters (or transcriptions), photos from training sessions/workshops, and certificates.
*   **Testimonials Carousel:**
    *   Elegant slider featuring quotes from executives and leaders.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* 3-5 real quotes from clients or partners.

## 7. Contact Page (`/contact`)
**Objective:** A professional, streamlined gateway for starting conversations.

*   **Inquiry Form:**
    *   Clean, minimalist form fields (Name, Organization, Inquiry Type, Message).
    *   Inquiry Type Dropdown: Strategic Partnerships, High-Level Consulting, Collaborations, Speaking Opportunities.
*   **Direct Contact Information:**
    *   Professional email and LinkedIn links.
    *   *Real Info Needed:* The specific email address and social links to be used.
