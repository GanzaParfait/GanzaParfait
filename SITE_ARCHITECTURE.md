# Website Structure

## Home

The entry point. It must immediately establish authority and overview Prince's value proposition.
* **Hero:** High-impact value statement and professional imagery.
* **Executive Summary:** A concise overview of Prince's strategic identity.
* **Impact Highlights:** Quick, quantifiable metrics of success.
* **Organizations:** Logos of trusted partners and clients.
* **Featured Work:** Selected, high-level case studies.
* **Philosophy:** Core beliefs on technology and operations.
* **Call To Action:** Clear pathways to contact or view deep dives.

---

## About

A deep dive into the person behind the impact.
* **Story:** The personal and professional background.
* **Journey:** Key milestones that shaped his current expertise.
* **Principles:** Fundamental operational and strategic beliefs.
* **Values:** Core ethical and professional drivers.
* **Vision:** Long-term goals and strategic focus.
* **Future Direction:** What he is building next.

## Impact

The proof of value. This section demonstrates real, measurable outcomes.
* **Organizations Served:** Detailed list of clients.
* **Systems Delivered:** High-level overviews of implemented solutions.
* **Training Initiatives:** Capacity building and educational impact.
* **Consulting Engagements:** Strategic advisory roles.
* **Research Contributions:** Contributions to data and organizational knowledge.

## Experience

A curated, professional timeline highlighting leadership and execution.

Examples:
* **Lerony**
* **Ethical Research Solutions**
* **Caritas Rwanda**
* **APN African Marketplace**

Each experience entry must rigorously focus on:
* **Challenge:** The core problem faced by the organization.
* **Contribution:** Prince's specific strategic and technical input.
* **Outcome:** The measurable result of the intervention.
* **Evidence:** Links to systems, reports, or testimonials.

## Projects

**Critical note:** This is not a standard developer project gallery. It is a comprehensive case-study collection.

Each project must contain a detailed narrative:
* **Context:** The environment and background.
* **Challenge:** The specific obstacle to overcome.
* **Approach:** The strategic methodology applied.
* **Solution:** The technological or operational fix implemented.
* **Outcome:** The business results achieved.
* **Lessons Learned:** Reflective insights demonstrating thought leadership.
* **Screenshots:** Premium, high-quality visuals of the final system.
