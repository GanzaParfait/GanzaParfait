# DESIGN_SYSTEM.md

# Design Direction

## Theme

Premium Light Theme

Avoid dark mode as the primary experience.

The website should feel:

* Modern
* Clean
* Executive
* Sophisticated
* Trustworthy
* Premium
* Minimal

---

## Inspiration

Think:

* Founder website
* Executive profile
* Modern consulting firm
* Premium technology company
* Thought leadership platform

Not:

* Freelancer portfolio
* Developer showcase
* Hacker aesthetic
* Gaming design

---

## Typography

Primary Font:

Outfit

Characteristics:

* Clean
* Modern
* Professional
* Readable

---

## Visual Style

Large spacing.

Strong typography.

Minimal distractions.

Elegant animations.

Subtle motion.

Generous white space.

Premium image presentation.

High quality project showcases.

---

## Color Direction

Primary:

White backgrounds

Secondary:

Soft gray surfaces

Accent:

Deep blue or premium neutral accent

Avoid:

Neon colors

Excessive gradients

Overly saturated palettes

---

## Animation Philosophy

Animations should communicate quality.

Never animate for decoration only.

Use animation for:

* Section reveals
* Scroll transitions
* Statistics
* Timeline progression
* Page transitions
* Hover interactions

Motion should feel intentional and professional.
